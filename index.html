Framework 01  ·  Live
ORBIT™  The Business AI Operating Framework

ORBIT is a five-step thinking system that helps business leaders apply AI to real business outcomes. Instead of starting with tools or prompts, ORBIT starts with strategy — defining what the business actually needs first, then determining how AI can help deliver it.

O
Outcome
Defines the specific 90-day business target — revenue, clients, or growth. Every AI decision anchors here.
R
Revenue Lever
Identifies whether the business needs to Acquire, Retain, or Expand. This one question changes everything.
B
Bottleneck
Diagnoses the single constraint blocking the outcome. Fix the real problem — not the symptoms.
I
Instruct
Only after O, R, and B are complete is the AI directive written — informed by strategy, not guesswork.
T
Track
Defines metrics, review cadence, and success criteria before launch. Measurement is built in from day one.

ORBIT gives any business leader a structured way to use AI that ties every action to a measurable outcome — replacing trial-and-error prompting with a repeatable system that improves over time.

Framework 02  ·  Live
PITCH-XI™  The Structured Prompt Framework

PITCH-XI is a seven-layer prompt framework that helps executives and business leaders get high-quality, actionable outputs from AI on their first attempt — without re-prompting or receiving generic results. Each layer removes a specific source of prompt failure.

P
Persona
Assigns AI a specific expert role — shifting it from generalist to specialist
I
Instruction
States the precise task — a specific action with a defined deliverable
T
Task Context
Provides the real business environment: size, tools, what's been tried
C
Constraints
Sets explicit guardrails — what not to do, format rules, hard limits
H
Hook
A reference point to calibrate against — an example of quality output
X
Execution
Defines the reasoning steps AI follows — a structured logic path
I
Intelligence
Validates output meets business-level criteria — ROI, bandwidth, feasibility
Where PITCH-XI helps most
Strategy roadmaps — board-ready AI strategy in one structured prompt
Financial summaries — CFO-level analysis without generic output
Risk assessments — severity-ranked, plain-English decision briefs
Who uses it
CEOs and founders at $5M–$500M companies building AI strategy
Senior executives who need first-prompt accuracy for high-stakes decisions
Consultants delivering AI strategy engagements to leadership teams
